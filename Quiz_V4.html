The stages of game

This game is a knowledge tester. There are 10 stages with questions of different level of difficulty.


Each correct answer allows the player to pass to the next round.

Categories

As it easily can be understood, this is a general knowledge quiz. There is a random system to choose the category of question. Just spin the roulette and one category will be chosen randomly.

Point System

The purpose of the player is to go as far as possible without making a mistake.
==> Lose the game
NOTE: The points are not related to real money despite having € in their name.

What do helps do

!HINT! By using hint help, you get some extra info about the correct answer.
!PUBLIC'S VOTE! This will show the answer that a group of people have voted the most.
!50:50! This will automaticly eleminate two of the incorrect alternatives.
You can use each help once per game.
